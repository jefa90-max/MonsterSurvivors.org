About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive survival game where you must fight against endless waves of monsters. Control your character, collect power-ups, and see how long you can survive in this challenging adventure.

The game features simple controls but deep strategic gameplay. Upgrade your character, unlock new abilities, and defeat powerful bosses as you progress through the levels.

Game Features

  • Endless waves of unique monsters
  • Multiple character classes with different abilities
  • Power-ups and upgrades to enhance your character
  • Intuitive controls for both desktop and mobile
  • Challenging boss fights
  • Progression system with unlockable content

How to Play

Desktop Controls: Use WASD or arrow keys to move. Mouse to aim and shoot. Number keys or click icons to use special abilities.

Mobile Controls: Touch joystick on the left to move. Use the buttons on the right to attack and activate special abilities.

Survive as long as possible while defeating enemies and collecting experience to level up!

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on movement and positioning to avoid getting surrounded
  • Prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle
  • Don't ignore defensive abilities - they can be lifesavers
  • Some weapons are better against certain enemy types
  • Try different character classes to find one that suits you
